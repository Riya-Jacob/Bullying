How To Solve Bullying?

There are various ways in which people can help to solve this particular issue of bullying. There are few ways that are listed here.

  • Educate Your Community - Help people understand bullying, how it is a serious issue in the society and how victims tend to suffer various mental illnesses. People who know what bullying is can understand and indetify it better.
  • Encourage Bystanders to Become "Upstanders" - Upstanders are people who stand up for themselves and others. Model ways for young people to intervene and speak up. Practice with role-playing.
  • Recognize and Respond - Bullying and intolerance manifest as verbal, written or physical acts that harm another person.
  • Foster Safety and Inclusion - Foster identify safe and welcoming environments that promote inclusion and acceptance, places where students feel everyone is respected and their identity is valued.
